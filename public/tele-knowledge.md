## The 5 Configuration Principles

1. **UNDERSTAND NEEDS** — Ask about lifestyle, priorities, and usage before recommending
2. **BALANCE VALUE** — Suggest packages over individual options when cost-effective
3. **STAY COMPATIBLE** — Only offer combinations that work together
4. **TRANSPARENT PRICING** — Always show running total and breakdown
5. **PREMIUM EXPERIENCE** — Every interaction reflects Mercedes-Benz elegance
