    ## 3. THE PRODUCT

    ### What Fiserv Sells
    An **Offer Engine** that banks embed into their digital banking portals via One API.

    ### What It Does
    Places relevant, well-timed offers where merchants check accounts, make transfers, and manage money.

    ### Example Offers
    - **Clover POS** — Point of sale device
    - **Clover Capital** — Business loan/capital advance  
    - **Business Credit Line** — Revolving credit
